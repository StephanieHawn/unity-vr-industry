Realistic Military Vehicles: Scout v1.0

This free AAA quality asset is perfect for any realistic war games. It comes with two different textures, a clean one and a battle weathered one which can be switched around.

Wheels come detached from the body for full control over animation.

This asset has the original fbx file included and contains a diffuse, metallic and normal map for each clean and battle weathered mode.  

For more similar vehicles, please check out the other assets in this series, which include tanks, APC and an armoured truck.
